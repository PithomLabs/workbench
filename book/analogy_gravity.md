## Newton → SR → GR under EBP v2.1

The historical sequence is a near-perfect EBP case study because it shows a theory promoted, partially demoted by new debt, replaced by a better-funded theory — which itself carries open debt today. No theory dies. Scope narrows honestly.

---

## Door → Workbench → Promoted: Newton

**Door.** Newton enters: *gravity is an inverse-square force between masses.* Owner: Newton. Claim: F = Gm₁m₂/r². No mechanism required at entry.

**Workbench.** Debt accumulates and gets worked:
- needMap retired: domain = (mass pair, separation), codomain = force magnitude, translation rule = inverse-square law. Kepler's laws derived as consequences.
- needToyCheck retired: planetary orbits, tides, projectile motion all agree quantitatively.
- needInvariant retired: conservation of energy and momentum survive across the gravitational interaction.
- needNullModel retired: the null model is uniform motion (no gravity). Newton beats it decisively across all then-measurable phenomena.
- needObstruction: Newton explicitly names it himself — *hypotheses non fingo*. He knows action-at-a-distance has no physical mechanism. He files the obstruction honestly and accepts the downgrade: "I describe, I do not explain." This is valid EBP retirement — state the obstruction and its consequence.
- needFaithfulnessReview: does F = Gm₁m₂/r² faithfully capture gravitational attraction? For point masses in inertial frames: yes. Contested for extended bodies, rotating frames. Stated and accepted.

**Promoted.** Principia (1687). All then-payable debt retired. No final-truth language. Newton himself avoids it.

## New Evidence Creates New Debt: Mercury

1859. Le Verrier calculates Mercury's perihelion precession. Newton predicts 532 arcsec/century. Observed: 575 arcsec/century. Gap: **43 arcsec/century**. Precise, reproducible, unexplained.

**needToyCheck refires on a promoted theory.** The finite test — Mercury's orbit — is a case Newton's framework cannot close. This is exactly EBP principle 5: new evidence creates new debt. Newton is not killed. He is **demoted to a scoped claim**: valid where v << c and gravitational potential Φ << c². The 43 arcsec gap is the ledger entry that stays open until GR.

---

## SR Opens needObstruction on Newton

1905. Special Relativity establishes c as a universal speed limit. Newton's gravity is instantaneous — a gravitational disturbance propagates at infinite speed.

**needObstruction fires on Newton's promoted theory.** Known blocker: Lorentz invariance. Newton's action-at-a-distance is inconsistent with SR. This is not a vague worry — it is a structural incompatibility. Consequence: Newton's theory cannot be valid in any regime involving dynamical gravitational sources and finite-speed signal propagation.

Newton moves from Promoted back to Workbench-with-debt. Not dead. **Scoped:** still valid for slow masses in weak fields where the instantaneous approximation holds. GPS base orbital mechanics uses Newton. The demotion is honest, not total.

---

## GR Enters the Door

Einstein's claim: *gravity is spacetime curvature; free-fall is geodesic motion; matter curves geometry via Gμν = 8πG Tμν.*

**Fresh idea at the Door.** Full debt load. No promotion yet.

---

## GR Works the Workbench

**needMap.** Domain = matter-energy distribution (stress-energy tensor Tμν), codomain = spacetime curvature (Einstein tensor Gμν), translation rule = Einstein field equations. Map explicitly written.

**needInvariant.** What survives Newton → GR? The weak-field, low-velocity limit. GR linearized + v << c + Φ << c² recovers Newton's F = Gm/r² exactly. The invariant is precise and checkable. What does *not* survive: absolute simultaneity, instantaneous propagation, Euclidean spatial geometry. Stated explicitly. No sleight of hand.

**needToyCheck.** Three finite tests:

- Mercury perihelion: GR predicts exactly 43 arcsec/century. The 56-year-old ledger entry closes.
- Light deflection (Eddington, 1919): Newton (treating light as particle) predicts 0.875 arcsec around the Sun. GR predicts 1.75 arcsec. Observed: 1.75 arcsec. Null model explicitly beaten on the same observable.
- Gravitational redshift: Pound-Rebka (1959) confirms GR's prediction at 1% precision.

All three toy checks pass. needToyCheck retired.

**needNullModel.** Newton is the null model. GR must reproduce Newton where Newton works and beat Newton where Newton fails. It does both. Null model retired — not by dismissing Newton but by containing him as a limit.

**needObstruction.** Two blockers stated honestly:

1. **Singularities.** GR predicts its own breakdown — the field equations produce singularities at black hole centers and the Big Bang. These are not physical predictions; they are signals that the theory exceeds its own domain of validity. Consequence: GR is incomplete at Planck-scale curvatures. Downgrade accepted. GR is not a theory of everything; it is a theory of classical spacetime geometry.

2. **Quantum incompatibility.** GR is not renormalizable as a quantum field theory. Known blocker when attempting to quantize gravity. Consequence: GR and the Standard Model cannot both be fundamental — at least one requires modification at high energies. Open obstruction. Not retired. GR remains promoted in its classical domain; the Planck-scale regime is explicitly out of scope.

**needFaithfulnessReview.** Does "gravity as geodesic deviation in curved spacetime" faithfully match the intended claim? For test particles (structureless, non-spinning): yes — geodesic equation recovers Newtonian gravity in the correct limit. For spinning, extended bodies: Papapetrou equations are needed; the geodesic formalization is not faithful. Stated. Contested in that regime. Promotion holds for the test-particle domain; the extended-body domain carries an open faithfulness note.

## No Final-Truth Claim — The Binding Constraint

Newton never claimed final truth explicitly and was vindicated for the restraint. Einstein's GR is sometimes spoken of as though it is final — that language is always wrong under EBP. GR is promoted because its current debt is retired within its stated scope. The moment a gravitational wave observation or a black hole shadow measurement produces a 5σ deviation from GR predictions, new debt opens and the promotion is revisited. Not shameful. That is the protocol working correctly.

## The Through-Line

Newton is not wrong. GR is not final. Each is a promoted idea within an honest scope, carrying whatever debt currently remains unpaid. The 43 arcsec gap sat open on the ledger for 56 years — not as a failure, but as a precisely stated next move waiting for someone with the right tools. Einstein had the tools. The ledger made the target visible.
