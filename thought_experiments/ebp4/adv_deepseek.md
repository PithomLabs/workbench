## Recommended Rewrite Principles

1. **Never present an interpretation (Copenhagen, Bohmian) as a physical mechanism** unless explicitly labelled as speculative and non‑consensus.
2. **For every claim beginning with “Bohmian mechanics [solves/explains/unifies]”, add:** “This is a minority, speculative view; no consensus exists.”
3. **Distinguish three categories clearly:**  
   - Established experimental fact  
   - Mainstream theoretical framework (with caveats)  
   - Speculative / fringe / toy model
4. **Source hygiene:** Eliminate YouTube, Instagram, Reddit, Medium as scientific sources. Use peer‑reviewed articles, collaboration white papers, or standard textbooks.
5. **When discussing quantum gravity, always state:** “No quantum gravity theory has experimental support; all are speculative.”
