## 1. Top 5 Most Dangerous Overclaims
1. **"Bohmian mechanics... bypassing the famous Black Hole Information Paradox entirely."** (Item 7) — Presents a speculative/unsupported claim as a solved fact, ignoring standard obstructions (firewalls, energy conditions).
2. **"Bohmian mechanics... directly accounts for the fading... dark energy signatures observed by DESI."** (Item 2) — Falsely connects a niche interpretation to cutting-edge experimental data, implying a confirmed link where none exists.
3. **"Copenhagen framework... means the definitive destruction of all quantum information."** (Items 6, 7, 8) — Incorrectly blames an interpretation for a fundamental physical conflict (Unitarity vs. GR) that affects *all* quantum theories.
4. **"The Bohmian Fix."** (Item 9) — Frames a speculative model as a definitive resolution to the Big Bang singularity.
5. **"Perfect Information Retrieval."** (Item 7) — Claims information can be reconstructed from Bohmian trajectories without explaining the physical mechanism for retrieval from a black hole.

## 3. Recommended Rewrite Principles
1. **Label Speculation:** Every claim about Bohmian mechanics solving cosmological problems must be prefixed with "In speculative models..." or "Proponents suggest..."
2. **Stop Blaming Copenhagen:** Remove the false dichotomy where Copenhagen fails physics and Bohm succeeds. Address the physics problems (singularities, information loss) as challenges for *all* quantum theories.
3. **Strict Source Hierarchy:** Remove YouTube, Instagram, Reddit, and Medium from scientific citations unless discussing pedagogy or public perception. Use primary literature or standard reviews.
4. **Distinguish Interpretation from Dynamics:** Bohmian mechanics is primarily an *interpretation* of standard QM. Extending it to cosmology requires *new dynamics* (e.g., modified WDW equations), which must be clearly stated.
5. **Check Links & Dates:** Remove future-dated/hallucinated links (2026).
