## Core correction pattern

Replace claims like:

> “Bohmian mechanics resolves X.”

with:

> “A Bohmian formulation may offer an observer-independent ontology for modeling X, but this remains speculative and does not currently solve the technical quantum-gravity problem.”

Replace:

> “DESI discovered weakening dark energy.”

with:

> “DESI found statistically interesting hints, strengthened by later analyses, that dark energy may evolve; confirmation still requires stronger evidence.”
